About this quiz

This quiz checks your understanding of the key ideas in bell hooks' Feminism Is for Everybody: Passionate Politics (South End Press, 2000). It covers her definition of feminism, the difference between reformist and revolutionary feminism, and her arguments about class, race, the body, masculinity, violence, and love.
